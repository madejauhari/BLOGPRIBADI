Green Store

Welcome to Green Store, the ultimate convenience destination where everything you need is just a step away. As a well-stocked minimarket, Green Store offers a vast selection of products across multiple categories to cater to every household and individual. In our grocery section, find fresh fruits and vegetables, dairy products, cooking essentials like rice, oils, and spices, and a variety of snacks, biscuits, and sweets for every craving. The beverage aisle offers everything from mineral water, juices, and soft drinks to a selection of coffee and tea for your daily energy boost.

Our household essentials section includes cleaning products, detergents, and toiletries, making it easy to keep your home in tip-top shape. We also carry a variety of baby products, from diapers and baby food to personal care items for the little ones. Looking for personal care items? Check out our range of soaps, shampoos, skincare products, and grooming essentials to meet your everyday hygiene needs.

At Green Store, we understand the importance of convenience in modern life, which is why we also stock practical items like LPG gas cylinders for your kitchen, gallon water refills to keep your household hydrated, and even cigarettes for those who require them. We’ve got a wide range of instant noodles, ready-to-eat meals, and frozen foods for quick and easy meal solutions. For those who need a last-minute top-up, our phone credit vouchers are also available, ensuring you’re never disconnected.

In addition to all of this, Green Store takes pride in offering a selection of everyday hardware items, small electronic accessories, and stationery, so whether it’s a new lightbulb, batteries, or school supplies, it’s all here. From last-minute groceries to essential household supplies, Green Store has it covered. Offering unbeatable convenience, Green Store is committed to making life easier and providing everything you need under one roof.


Green Laundry

Welcome to Green Laundry, where every garment gets the care it deserves. Offering a range of services to suit every need, Green Laundry makes laundry day effortless and convenient. Choose from our Economy, Executive, or Express options based on your schedule and budget. Whether it’s a quick refresh or premium care, we’ve got you covered.

At Green Laundry, flexibility is key. For those who need just a specific service, we offer ironing-only or wash-only options, giving customers the freedom to choose exactly what they need. Beyond regular laundry, we specialize in handling more delicate and bulky items such as curtains, stuffed toys, bed sheets, hats, and more, ensuring that even the toughest or most delicate items are treated with expert care.

Our Economy Service provides affordable laundry care for everyday wear, while the Executive Service adds an extra touch of quality, perfect for professional attire or special garments. And when time is of the essence, our Express Service guarantees a fast turnaround without compromising on quality.

At Green Laundry, every piece of clothing, from the simplest t-shirt to the largest curtain, is handled with precision and care. Experience hassle-free laundry with exceptional results, no matter the item.
